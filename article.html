  • Mail Art began in the 1960s when artists sent postcards inscribed with poems or drawings through the post rather than exhibiting or selling them through conventional commercial channels. Mail art and can take a variety of forms including postcards, packages, faxes, emails and blogs. It is considered to be the predecessor of net art.
